🔧 HOW TO CUSTOMIZE

  Different number of plans:
    AIDER_BENCH_NUM_PLANS=2 bash shared/scripts/run_multiplan.sh
    AIDER_BENCH_NUM_PLANS=6 bash shared/scripts/run_multiplan.sh

  Parallel execution (faster wall-clock time):
    AIDER_BENCH_THREADS=4 bash shared/scripts/run_multiplan.sh

  Specific language:
    AIDER_BENCH_LANGUAGES=python bash shared/scripts/run_multiplan.sh

  Fewer tasks for testing:
    AIDER_BENCH_NUM_TESTS=5 bash shared/scripts/run_multiplan.sh
