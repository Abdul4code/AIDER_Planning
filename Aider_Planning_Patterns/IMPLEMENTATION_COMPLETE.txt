================================================================================
KEY DESIGN DECISIONS
================================================================================

1. Temperature Sampling (vs. other strategies)
   - Self-Consistency proven effective in literature
   - No prompt engineering required
   - Compatible with all LLMs supporting temperature
   - Generates diverse solutions naturally

2. Majority Voting (vs. LLM-based ranking)
   - Test-based scoring (objective, not LLM-dependent)
   - No additional LLM calls for evaluation
   - Robust to outliers
   - Aligns with paper's Simple majority vote strategy

3. Sequential Execution (default)
   - Ensures predictable time budget
   - Simplicity (no race conditions)
   - Optional parallelization via AIDER_BENCH_THREADS
   - Fair comparison with baseline

4. Aggregated Reporting
   - Reports best plan outcome (for comparison with baseline)
   - Tracks total cost across all plans
   - Provides per-plan breakdown (for analysis)
   - Enables cost-quality tradeoff analysis

5. Fair Time Budget
   - Equal allocation: task_timeout / num_plans
   - Conservative minimum: 60 seconds per plan
   - Prevents any plan from overwhelming total time
   - Respects hard 15-minute per-task limit
